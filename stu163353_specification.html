YogaZenith Mini-Site Specification

Objective/Goals

The main goal of the YogaZenith website is to educate and motivate visitors about the practice of yoga and how it positively affects both mental health. Our aim is to develop a platform that offers everything individuals need to explore yoga discover interests stay active and stimulate their intellectual curiosity. The website will provide information, resources and a sense of belonging, guiding visitors on their yoga journey.

Key Audience(s)

The target audience for this Site:

  • Experienced Yoga Practitioners: These individuals are looking for knowledge and ongoing inspiration to enhance their yoga practice. The mini site will provide them with insights and resources to support their journey.
  • Beginners: People who are new, to yoga and want to start their journey will find the mini site a resource. It offers guidance and information to help them take their steps into the world of yoga.
  • Health Enthusiasts: Those interested in experiencing the health benefits of yoga such as stress reduction, improved flexibility and overall well being will discover insights and resources on how to enhance their wellness.
  • Intellectuals: Users intrigued by the spiritual aspects of yoga well as its cultural significance will find the mini site a source of engaging content that satisfies their intellectual curiosity.
  • Fitness Enthusiasts: Individuals who want to incorporate yoga into their fitness routines for an approach, towards health will benefit from guidance and resources on the mini site. These resources can help them achieve their fitness goals while maintaining well-being.

Provisional Site Structure

  1. Index Page:

    • Hero Section : "Discover Inner Peace and Wellness Through Yoga"
    • Welcome message and a "Get Started" button leading to the "About Yoga" page.
    • What We Offer" section featuring benefits of yoga and testimonials with interactive hover effects.
    • Navigation menu.

  2. About Yoga Page:

    • Information about the origin of yoga.
    • Introduction to the concept of yoga.
    • Exploration of yoga in mind and spirit.
    • Types of yoga with engaging hover effects.
    • Inspirational quotes by respected figures.

  3. Benefits of Yoga Page:

    • Nine key benefits of yoga presented in a grid format with interactive hover effects.

  4. Testimonials Page:

    • Displaying testimonials from individuals who have benefited from YogaZenith.
    • Beautifully aligned testimonials in a grid format.

  5. Contact Us:

    • A contact form for users to ask questions about health and yoga.
    • Fields for name, email, phone number, source of referral, and a message area.
    • Submit button with hover effect.

Functional Specification

  • Navigation:A menu that's easy for users to navigate, with a design that transforms into a hamburger menu on screens.
  • Forms: The contact form will include validation to ensure accurate and complete user submissions.These forms should collect necessary information (name, email,phone number,how did you know about us, message) and include validation.
  • Engaging Hover Effects: Exciting hover effects will be incorporated into pages such as "What We Offer" and "Benefits of Yoga" to create an interactive user experience.
  • Flexible Grid Alignment: CSS flexbox will be utilized to align content in a format on pages that display grids.
  • Responsive Design:The website will adjust its layout. Design to fit screen sizes and devices ensuring that users have the best possible experience.
